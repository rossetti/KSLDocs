fun matTwoPowModM(A: Array<DoubleArray>, B: Array<DoubleArray>, m: Double, e: Int)

Computes (A raised to (2 to e)) mod m and puts the result in B. B = A^{2^e} Works even if A = B.

the matrix to raise to a power
